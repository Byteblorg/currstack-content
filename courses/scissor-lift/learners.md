# Scissor Lift Course
Learner Guide v0.1

Safety comes first.
## Learning Objectives

After completing OSL course, learners will be able to:

- Identify key hazards and risks related to the task
- Apply safe operating procedures and relevant controls
- Demonstrate correct pre-use inspection and operational checks
- Respond appropriately to abnormal and emergency situations


## Assessment

Learners will be assessed using:

- Knowledge check (MCQ / short answers) covering key safety rules and procedures
- Practical performance checklist (if applicable) aligned to the lesson plan
- Pass criteria: Minimum required score + satisfactory demonstration of critical safety steps
